Training and Continuing Education Unit

Unit Duties

The Training and Continuing Education Unit at the center is responsible for organizing and implementing continuous training programs for scientists and researchers participating in genomic research. This unit aims to improve and develop the skills and knowledge necessary for excellence in the complex and evolving field of genomics. Educational courses, seminars, and specialized workshops are provided to enhance scientists' understanding of technology and modern methods in genome analysis and interpretation of genetic data.

Our Vision

The unit aims to provide distinguished training programs that cover a wide range of topics related to genomics, including advanced genetic analysis techniques and their applications in research, diagnosis, and treatment. The unit also seeks to provide opportunities for communication and collaboration among researchers and scientists in the field of genomics, both within the center and with other relevant institutions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support continuous learning and enhance scientific and professional excellence in the field of genomics through distinguished training programs and a stimulating learning environment. This contributes to advancing the field of genomics, improving healthcare, and enhancing scientific understanding of genetic composition.

Our Objectives

The objectives of the Training and Continuing Education Unit are to develop the scientific and technical knowledge and skills of scientists and researchers in the field of genomics.

The Programs and Activities

The Training and Continuing Education Unit at the Research Excellence Center for Medical Genomics offers specialized and comprehensive programs that cover the latest technologies and methods in genome analysis and interpretation of genetic data.
