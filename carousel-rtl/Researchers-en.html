Researchers

The CEGMR is dedicated to the advancement of medicinal sciences and translation of research findings into better health care. It is totally committed to explore fundamental molecular causes of diseases through discovery research and to device the innovative therapies of tomorrow by translational research. For this purpose a group of highly talented, experienced, dedicated and enthusiastic research leaders has been assembled to undertake innovative research in the areas of unmet medical needs in order to provide novel treatment and diagnostic options to the clinicians in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.
