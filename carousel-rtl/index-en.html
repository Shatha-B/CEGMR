Center of Excellence in Genomic Medicine Research

Welcome to the Center of Excellence in Genomic Medicine Research (CEGMR) at King Abdulaziz University.

More

Our Vision

To alleviate the suffering of people from genetic diseases in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia through leading innovative research to discover biological signs for personalized medicine adoption, thereby providing better healthcare.

Our Message

To be a distinguished center in medical genomics research, and to strive to keep up with the medical advancement and translate its research findings into improving healthcare. We also exert great efforts to provide a research environment that enhances the growth and collaboration of researchers, by being a national model for excellent research and education.
